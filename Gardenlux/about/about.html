    Welcome to GardenLy - Your Premier Garden Design and Construction Service

    At GardenLux, we take pride in offering top-notch garden design and construction services. With over 15 years of experience, Roy Laniado, our founder, brings a wealth of expertise and passion to every project.
    "My journey in the gardening industry began in the early 2000s, where I gained invaluable hands-on experience that has shaped my approach as a garden designer."
    Over the years, I have honed my skills and acquired numerous certifications in professional garden design, irrigation system planning, woodwork, and more...
    Combining this expertise with our extensive portfolio of successfully completed projects, ranging from small gardens to expansive landscapes, GardenLux delivers unparalleled garden planning solutions.
    • Customized landscape and vegetation planning tailored to your space and preferences.
    • Creation of inviting seating areas, rockeries, and charming spots throughout your garden.
    • Efficient planning of irrigation and lighting systems for optimal functionality and sustainability.
    • Expert installation of artificial grass to enhance the beauty and functionality of your outdoor space.
    • Comprehensive garden development services, including soil preparation and woodwork.
    Following our initial consultation, you'll receive a detailed quote based on your garden's unique requirements. We'll then work closely with you to refine the design, ensuring it meets your family's needs for hosting, play, and relaxation.

    Our dedicated team is comprised of skilled professionals who share our commitment to excellence. From meticulous plantings to precise stone placements, we ensure every aspect of your project is executed with care and precision.
